What is MadHack?

Introducing Sri Lankas first Mobile Application Development Hackathon, the IEEE Student Branch of UCSC proudly presents 'MadHack', an inter-university hackathon followed by a series of workshops. MadHack aims to let the participants put their creativity and innovation to the test by giving them the opportunity to develop in any platform of choice.

Elevating Mobile App Development

MadHack is a premier hackathon and workshop series for mobile app development in Sri Lanka. Participants can showcase their creativity and learn from industry experts. Whether you're a beginner or experienced developer, MadHack offers something for everyone. Join us and take the next step in your mobile app development journey.

Timeline

January 28th

Launch Event

We will be officially kicking off and opening registrations. Join our events and stay connected with our social media to know the latest updates. See you soon

February 11th

Workshop I

This is the first workshop, which will be held online and cover the topic of Android Native. Registrations will also open for the physical workshop on the same day.

February 18th

Workshop II

This is the second workshop, which will be held physically and cover topics such as Flutter, React Native, Low code, and Deployment. Registrations will also open for the competition on the same day.

February 25th

6 hour hackathon

This is a 6-hour hackathon event where participants can showcase their skills and build projects in a limited time frame.

February 26th

Finalists Announcing

The finalists will be announced after the 6-hour hackathon event. The finalists will be given a chance to participate in the 24-hour hackathon event.

March 04th

24 hour hackathon

This is a 24-hour hackathon event where participants can showcase their skills and build projects in a longer time frame.

March 05th

Final Event

This is the final event where participants will present their projects and the award ceremony will be held to recognize the best projects.
